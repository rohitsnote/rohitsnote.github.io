Rohit's Note

Light in the Dark


This is the world where the Sun shines bright;
And the same world where the darkness rules nights!

If shadow dwells in light then what’s surprise?
Then “heaven in hell” may be right as well.

Then why in bad times when your heart goes dark-
You care not the least to light a spark!

Why for joy, on time, you rely?
Haven’t you ever noticed stars in the sky?
